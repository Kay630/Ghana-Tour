1

Bookings & Reservations

A booking is confirmed once Wi-Ann Travel & Tour has received the required deposit and issued a written confirmation with a booking reference number.

  • All bookings are subject to availability at the time of confirmation.
  • The lead traveller must be at least 18 years of age and is responsible for all members of the party.
  • It is your responsibility to ensure all travel documents (passport, visa, vaccination certificates) are valid and up to date.
  • Special requests (dietary requirements, accessibility needs, room preferences) must be communicated at time of booking. While we will make every reasonable effort, these cannot be guaranteed.
  • Bookings made online are provisional until confirmed in writing by our team, typically within 24 hours.
2

Payments & Pricing

All prices are quoted in US Dollars (USD) unless otherwise stated. Prices include the services specified in the tour itinerary and exclude items listed as optional or personal expenses.

  • Deposit: A non-refundable deposit of 30% is required to secure your booking.
  • Full Payment: The remaining balance is due at least 14 days before the tour departure date.
  • Group Tours: Full payment may be required at time of booking for certain group tour packages — this will be specified in the tour listing.
  • Payment Methods: We accept bank transfers, card payments (Visa, Mastercard), and mobile money (MTN, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo). Bank transfer details will be provided upon booking confirmation.
  • Price Changes: Prices are locked once a deposit has been paid. However, increases may apply for fuel surcharges, government taxes, or currency fluctuations and you will be notified promptly.
Tip: Booking at least 3 weeks in advance secures the best available pricing and availability.
3

Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellations must be submitted in writing to info@wi-anntravel.com. The following cancellation and refund policy applies:

  • 90 days or more before the tour: Full refund of the booking amount.
  • 42–89 days before the tour: 50% refund of the booking amount.
  • Less than 30 days before the tour: No refund will be issued.
  • Refund Processing: Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.
  • Bank & Card Fees: Customers are responsible for any bank or card issuer fees that may apply during refund processing.

Wi-Ann Travel & Tour strongly recommends purchasing comprehensive travel insurance that covers cancellation costs, medical expenses, and personal belongings.

If Wi-Ann Travel & Tour cancels a tour due to circumstances within our control, a full refund or alternative date will be offered. We are not liable for cancellations caused by force majeure events (natural disasters, government orders, pandemics, etc.).

Traveller Conduct

We expect all travellers to treat our guides, staff, local communities, and fellow travellers with respect. Wi-Ann Travel & Tour reserves the right to remove any traveller from a tour without refund if their behaviour is deemed disruptive, offensive, or a risk to others.

  • Consumption of excessive alcohol or illegal substances during tours is prohibited.
  • Photography at sacred or restricted sites must follow posted guidelines and guide instructions.
  • Littering or damage to natural or cultural heritage sites may result in removal from the tour and/or legal liability.

While we exercise reasonable care in selecting partners, we cannot be held liable for acts or omissions of third parties beyond our direct control.

Itinerary Changes

While every effort is made to deliver the published itinerary, certain factors — weather, road conditions, park access, local events — may require adjustments.

  • Significant changes will be communicated as early as possible, with alternatives offered where feasible.
  • Wi-Ann Travel & Tour reserves the right to substitute guides or vehicles of equivalent or superior quality.

Disputes & Governing Law

Privacy Policy

Wi-Ann Travel & Tour ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your data when you interact with our website or book our services.

Summary: We only collect data we need to process your booking and improve your experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
8

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide to us and information gathered automatically during your use of our website:

  • Identity & Contact: Full name, email address, phone number, nationality.
  • Booking Details: Tour preferences, travel dates, number of participants, special requirements.
  • Payment Information: Card details (processed securely; we do not store full card numbers) or mobile money number.
  • Correspondence: Emails, enquiry forms, and messages you send us.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and session duration (via cookies and analytics tools).
9

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  1. Processing and confirming your tour bookings.
  2. Communicating booking updates, itinerary details, and receipts.
  3. Responding to enquiries and providing customer support.
  4. Sending promotional offers and newsletters (only with your consent).
  5. Improving our website experience and tour offerings.
  6. Meeting legal and regulatory obligations.
Marketing Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email or contacting us directly.
10

  • Legal Authorities: Where required by Ghanaian law or court order.

All third-party partners are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@wi-anntravel.com. We will respond within 14 working days.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g., booking session data).
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics — anonymised).
  • Preference Cookies: To remember settings such as language preferences.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

13

Data Security

We take your data security seriously. Our website uses 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission. Payment information is processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors and is not stored on our servers beyond what is required for transaction records.

Despite our efforts, no internet transmission is completely secure. We encourage you to protect your own account credentials and notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorised use of your data.

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Booking records are retained for a minimum of 5 years for accounting and legal purposes.
