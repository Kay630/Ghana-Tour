Kumasi – Ashanti Region

Discover the heartland of the mighty Ashanti Kingdom. Visit Manhyia Palace, Kejetia Market — Africa's largest open market — and the vibrant kente weaving villages of Bonwire and Adanwomase. Our team will work with you to create the perfect itinerary tailored to your interests, travel style, and budget. With our affordable group tour packages or customized private journeys, the Ashanti Kingdom awaits you.

Volta Region & Northern Ghana

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Ghana's Volta Region and Northern savanna with us. From the thundering Wli Waterfalls — West Africa's tallest — to the wildlife of Mole National Park and the ancient Larabanga Mosque, we've got you covered. Choose from our affordable group tour packages or let us tailor a trip specifically for you. With options to fit every interest and budget, an exceptional Ghanaian adventure awaits.
