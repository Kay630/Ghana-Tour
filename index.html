Kumasi – Ashanti Region

Step into the soul of the legendary Ashanti Kingdom. Stroll through the historic Manhyia Palace, lose yourself in the bustling stalls of Kejetia Market — one of Africa's largest open-air markets — and witness the centuries-old art of kente weaving in the villages of Bonwire and Adanwomase. Whether you choose a guided group tour or a private journey built around your schedule and interests, the golden heritage of Ashanti is waiting to welcome you.

Volta Region & Northern Ghana

From the thundering cascade of Wli Waterfalls — the tallest in West Africa — to the sweeping savannas of Mole National Park and the ancient mud walls of the Larabanga Mosque, Ghana's Volta and Northern regions offer an adventure unlike any other. We'll build you an experience that fits your budget and curiosity, whether that's an affordable group outing or a fully personalized expedition into Ghana's most spectacular landscapes.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know before booking your Ghana adventure with Wi-Ann Travel & Tour.

How do I book a tour with Wi-Ann Travel & Tour?

Booking is easy — simply click the "Book Now" or "Help Me Plan" button on any page, fill in your details, choose your tour and travel date, then complete payment. Our team will reach out within 24 hours to confirm your booking. You can also contact us directly via email or WhatsApp.

What payment options do you offer?

We offer two flexible payment options — full payment upfront, or a half-and-half installment plan where you pay 50% to secure your booking and the remaining balance before departure. We accept Visa, Mastercard, MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash, and AirtelTigo Money.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes. We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your departure date. You can also reschedule your tour to a future date at no additional charge. Simply contact our team and we'll sort it out for you.

Are your tours suitable for solo travellers?

Absolutely. Whether you're travelling solo, as a couple, with family, or in a group, we have options for everyone. Our group tours are a great way to meet fellow travellers, while our private tours give you full flexibility and personalised attention.

What is included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include a certified local guide, transportation within Ghana, entrance fees to attractions, and curated itinerary planning. Accommodation and international flights are not included unless specified. Contact us for a full breakdown of what's included in your chosen tour.

Do I need a visa to visit Ghana?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Citizens of ECOWAS countries do not require a visa. Most other nationalities can apply for a Ghana e-Visa online before arrival. We recommend checking with your local Ghanaian embassy or the Ghana Immigration Service website for the latest requirements.
